TOUR INFORMATION

We run tours every day, if you want to join us please come down to the reception and ask to book. Please try to book one day ahead as there can be limited spaces.

BEACH TOURS

Tour to Ostional Beach Wildlife Refuge and San Juanillo beach:

Every day at 9am just after breakfast we will go by car on a half a day trip to Ostional Beach, this national refuge is thought to be the 2nd most important nesting site in Costa Rica. This location is home to at least three different species of sea turtles while they go through the dangerous and laborious process of reproducing. Afterwards we will head to San Juanillo beach to enjoy its crystal clear waters, where we will spend the rest of the trip chilling, swimming and snorkeling.

BOAT TOUR

Boat Tour to San Juanillo beach:

Every day at 9am just after breakfast we will go by boat on a tour to San Juanillo beach. On the way we will do a bit of fishing and after arriving in San Juanillo we can enjoy its crystal clear waters, where we will spend the rest of the trip chilling, swimming and snorkeling.
