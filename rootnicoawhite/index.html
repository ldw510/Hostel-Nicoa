THE STORY OF NICOA

THE STORY OF NICOA

This is the story of Nicoa El Cacique Chorotega. (A native king from the chorotega region). Nicoa was the last great ruler of the Chorotega lineage and the father of the princess Nosara. Cacique Nicoya was a descendant of the first Chorotegas who arrived in what is now Guanacaste and who conquered all the towns in that region, including the Corobicíes of South American tradition.
He was lord and master of all the Chorotega peoples. Upon their arrival in 1522, the Spanish conquerors led by Captain Gil González Dávila were able to confirm Nicoya's supremacy over other caciques and his name lives on today in the second canton of Guanacaste, a gulf and peninsula.
Regarding the origin of its name it is traditionally attributed roots of the Nahuatl language, the one spoken by the Aztecs and its meaning is "way of the warriors" or "corn on one side and the other".

ABOUT US

ABOUT US

Nicoa was founded by Federico in 2020. Federico has travelled around the world from his native Costa Rica and stayed in every type of hostel you can imagine.
He loved his time abroad discovering new cultures and wanted to bring something back to his homeland.
Nicoa was always a dream, made by a traveller for other travellers. Named after a native Costa Rican ruler who resisted the Spanish conquest.
Nicoa represents a safe space for all.
