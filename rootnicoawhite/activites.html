ACTIVITY INFORMATION

We run a different activity every night, if you want to join us please come down to the communal area at 6:45pm. We also run activities during the day and for more information please ask at reception.

MONDAY: CARIBBEAN NIGHT

Monday Caribbean night.
Every Monday at 6:45pm we get together and enjoy the traditional Caribbean dish of rice and beans with chicken or vegetables in a coconut sauce.

TUESDAY: TACO TUESDAY

Taco Tuesday.
Every Tuesday at 6:45pm we head to the hottest Mexican restaurant in town and go for a dinner date of tacos, juice or beers and the beloved holy mexican water (Tequila)!

WEDNESDAY: PASTA NIGHT

Wednesday pasta night.
Every Wednesday at 6:45pm we gather and enjoy some delicious pasta made by the Hostel Staff. You can choose either a meat or vegetable bolognese. Everyone gets a FREE dinner!!!

THURSDAY: OUTDOOR MOVE NIGHT

Thursday outdoor movie night.
Every Thursday night after dinner we go and enjoy the outdoor cinema showing a Latin American Independent Film. Of course everyone gets FREE popcorn!!

FRIDAY: BBQ

Friday BBQ.
Every Friday at 6:45pm we hang out by the pool and have a nice bbq (vegetarian friendly) enjoy some music, drinks and play beer pong.

SATURDAY: PIZZA NIGHT

Saturday pizza night.
Every Saturday at 6:45pm we get together and order some pizzas, have a few bevs and relax.

SUNDAY: EUROPEAN MOVIE NIGHT

Sunday movie night.
Every Sunday after dinner we enjoy the outdoor cinema showing a European Independent Film. Everyone gets FREE popcorn!!

SURF CAMP

Surf Camp: If your looking to spend your holiday chilling and learning how to surf Nosara its the spot for you, let us at Hostel Nicoa take care of it! Come learn how to surf with the locals and enjoy our Costa Rican hospitality.

YOGA RETREAT

Yoga retreats: Nicoa is the perfect spot to organize your retreat, in a 1000mts2 (3250ft) property there is plenty of green space, where you can practice out door yoga by the pool and host activities at night.

VOLUNTEERING

Volunteering Program:
Join our volunteering program planting trees, cleaning beaches, help at the Ostional Wildlife Refuge at the Turtle nesting or just come teach English at the local elementary school.
